# Sensitive Wording Reference

Use this for public-facing articles when the user asks for review, publication, or neutral wording.

## Political / Regional

| Avoid | Prefer | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 国产替代 | 国内厂商 | More neutral wording |
| 数据不出境 | 数据存储在境内 | More precise |
| 网络访问不便 | 特殊网络环境 | Avoids unnecessary sensitivity |

## Commercial Neutrality

| Avoid | Prefer | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 贵 | 费用较高 | Less subjective |
| 便宜 | 性价比高 | More neutral/positive |
| 坑 | 体验不佳 | Avoids emotional labeling |

## Data Security

| Avoid | Prefer | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 个人数据主权 | 数据由用户掌控 | More concrete |
| 隐私泄露 | 数据安全风险 | More professional when risk is not confirmed |

## Technical Claims

| Avoid | Prefer | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 自动提取 | best-effort 机制沉淀 | Avoid overpromising |
| 自动生成 | 辅助生成 | Avoid overpromising |
| AI 取代 | AI 辅助 | More accurate in most cases |

## Absolute Claims

| Avoid | Prefer | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 最佳 | 较优 | Avoid absolute claim |
| 最强 | 性能领先 | Prefer measurable claim |
| 唯一 | 独特 | Avoid exclusionary claim |
| 完美 | 完善 | Avoid overclaiming |

## Time Claims

| Avoid | Prefer | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 永远 | 长期 | Avoid absolute time claim |
| 立即 | 快速 | Avoid hard timing promise |
| 马上 | 尽快 | Avoid hard timing promise |
